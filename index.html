Methodology:
· Mean pts/GW = total pts / GWs played. Remaining = 38 - GWs played.
· Weekly scores sampled from a Gamma distribution (always positive, right-skewed).
· Chip weeks (extreme outlier scores) automatically trimmed before calculating stats.
· Full Season = all GWs trimmed  |  Blended = 70% season + 30% last 6 GWs  |  Form Only = last 6 GWs.
· Avg Pos = mean finishing position across all sims (1 = best).
· Chip badges (TC/BB/FH/WC) show remaining chips fetched from FPL — factored into simulation.
· TC adds ~1x captain score to one GW · BB adds ~18 bench pts to one GW · FH prevents worst GW.
· Win % = % finishing highest  |  Last % = % finishing lowest.
